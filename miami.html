What are the benefits of data sharing initiatives?

Benefits - Discovery

  • Sharing data can help accelerate discovery of new insights
  • Empower more end users to analyze data in different ways

New Insights

Benefits - Consensus

  • Builds consensus around challenging questions
  • Forces organization to develop common methods
  • Makes analysis and reporting more authoritative

Benefits - Scale

  • Self-service analytics by non-quantitatively informed staff frees up analytics staff to work on more advanced projects

Benefits - Transparency

  • Staff must be more accountable for their work
  • Leads to better documentation of methodology

Benefits - Automation

  • Data sharing forces automation
  • Algorithms need to be scripted

Transition from Siloes to Federated Analytics

Transition - Initial Setbacks

  • Sharing data technically is not as much the problem as making sense of it
  • Analytics insights must connect with the core business issues
  • Business experts may have technical gaps
  • Need to meet in the middle

Transition - Training

  • Knowledge transfer
  • Growing pains with getting analytics staff up to speed with the business side (and vice-versa)
  • Requires veteran analysts to train other analysts on data elements and reporting methods

Transition - Data Dictionaries

  • Critical to have; however, often do not provide total insight into analytics operations

Transition - User Guides

  • Critical from an analytics perspective
  • Train staff how to use data
  • Create conversation around methodology
  • Work toward development of Analytics CoE
